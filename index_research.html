NaiRoM

Nature-inspired Robotic Motion

Our research fields:

Aerial Robotics: We draw inspiration from nature to study aerodynamics and flight dynamics, guiding the design of advanced aerial robots.(Related publications)

Aquatic Robotics: We draw inspiration from the fast, powerful swimming of tuna to develop robotic fish capable of high-speed propulsion, leaping out of the water, and delivering low-cost performance.(Related publications)

Multi-modal Robots: Inspired by Flying fish, we propose a bioinspired robotic design that integrates periodic caudal-fin propulsion underwater with deployable wings for zero-pitching aerial gliding.(Related publications)
